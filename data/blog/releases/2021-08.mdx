## Faster conversions

Fortunately, August has been a great month when it comes to progression. We updated our encoding dependencies and report a 25% increase in conversion performance.

## Content Preparations

A lot of preparations have been made in order to write content faster. More MDX, less code. Yey. We have also implemented the option to search through our articles on our archive page in real-time, since the number of articles will grow drastically.
